About Dabaski

Music Producer, Sound Designer, & Creative Artist

Who I Am

I'm Dabaski, a music producer and artist specializing in dubstep, color bass, and lofi hip hop. I started after wanting to make songs like big EDM producers such as Skrillex and IsaidMusic.

My Music

My discography includes:

  • PROMISE ME (2022) - Dubstep / Color Bass Album
  • The Future Ain't So Bad (2019) - LoFi Hip Hop Album
  • Meet Your Maker (2021) - Dubstep Single
  • PROMISE ME (VIP) (2021) - VIP Remix
  • Various flips and remixes of tracks by artists like God's Warrior and GUTTER

What I Do

  • Original music production (Dubstep, Color Bass, LoFi Hip Hop)
  • Sound design and synthesis
  • Remixes and flips
  • Music production tutorials and educational content
